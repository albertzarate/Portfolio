NutriVision - Food Recognition App

Worked with 2 other team members to create a food recognition app that shows nutrition facts at the tip of your fingers. Just take a photo of your food and NutriVision will immediately tell you the food's calories, fat, sodium, carbs, fiber, sugar, and protein content.

MP3 Player

Worked with 2 others to make an MP3 Player using LPC1758 microcontroller, VS1053b Audio Decoder, and 20x4 RGB LCD. The project was implemented in C and includes features such as 4 multi-functional buttons, a volume knob, auxilary input, 6000mA battery, and interactive menus.

Self-Driving Robot

Worked with 5 other team members to create self-driving robot that simulated care-bots. My main contribution was developing code in C and implementing IR sensors, bumper switches, servo motors, and ultrasonic sensors.

--C Programming Language

Worked with 2 others to create a programming language that resembles C but with added functionality from Python. The project was implemented in C++ and includes features such as if-elif-else statements, For-in loops, function and printf support, and integer bit indexing and setting.

LetsHang Web App

LetsHang is a web application that enables new or shy SJSU students to make friends by doing activities together. Choose between 3 activities to do and you will be matched with another user with the same choice. Depending on the choice made, a location will be sent to both users so they may meet up.
