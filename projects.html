NutriVision - Food Recognition App

Worked with 2 other team members to create a food recognition app that shows nutrition facts at the tip of your fingers. Just take a photo of your food and NutriVision will immediately tell you the food's calories, fat, sodium, carbs, fiber, sugar, and protein content. Front-end made in HTML5 and CSS3, server made using Flask and Python (hosted on Amazon EC2), and APIs used include Google Cloud Vision and Nutritionix.

GitHub | Architecture

MP3 Player

Worked with 2 others to make an MP3 Player using LPC1758 microcontroller, VS1053b Audio Decoder, and 20x4 RGB LCD. The project involved creating drivers for SPI and UART in C. Includes features such as reading from an SD card, 4 multi-functional buttons, a volume knob, auxilary input, 6000mA battery, displaying song information from tags, and interactive menu screens.
